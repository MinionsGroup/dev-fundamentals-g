Given two points: 

P1(x1,y1) and P2(x2,y2)

|Ax|=|(x2-x1)|

|Ay|=|(y2-y1)|
The Area is: 

Area=(|Ay|  |Ax|)/2

Algorithm
1 Read x1, y1, x2, y2, metric unit
2. Calculate:  Ax and Ay
3. Calculate: Area
4. show área

Caculate_sides (x1, y1, x2, y2); Ax, Ay
	Ax=|(x2-x1)|
	Ay=|(y2-y1)|
Calculate_area (Ax,Ay); Area
	Area=(|Ay|  |Ax|)/2
Show_area (Area)
Print (Area + metric unit+”^2”)
